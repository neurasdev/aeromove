Why AeroMove

AeroMove is built for people who want a dependable way to move large amounts of media between iPhone and Mac. It gives you visibility before starting, clear progress during transfer, and a smooth path for backing up photo and video libraries over your local network.

Built for large transfers

Select many photos and videos at once and let long transfers run reliably.

See time estimates early

View export time and transfer time before starting, so you know what to expect.

Smart storage protection

Avoid failed transfers when iPhone storage is low by checking available sending-side space during selection.

Perfect for backup

Great for backing up your iPhone photo and video library to your MacBook over your local network.
